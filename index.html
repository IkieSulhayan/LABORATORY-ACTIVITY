About the Event

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is an annual celebration of technology and innovation hosted by the College of Computer and Information Studies. This event brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals for a week of workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can attend SMCC-CCIS Week 2025?

A: All SMCC students, faculty, and staff are welcome to attend. Some events may require prior registration.

Q: Is there a registration fee?
